Privacy Policy

For Class 10 NCERT Maths Solutions

Effective Date: October 31, 2025

Litti choka studio ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Class 10 NCERT Maths Solutions mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Service.

We value your privacy. Our Service is designed to provide math solutions, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (like your name, address, or email) from you directly.

Information Collection and Use

Our Service is designed to be simple and useful. We do not require you to sign up or provide any personally identifiable information. The information we do collect or store is used to improve the app and support its free-of-charge model.

1. Information Stored Locally (On Your Device)

To provide a better experience, our app saves some data directly on your device using Android's "SharedPreferences". This data is never sent to us and remains private to you on your device. This includes:

  • Theme Preference: To remember if you have selected Light Mode or Dark Mode.
  • In-App Review Timestamp: To remember when we last asked you for a review, so we don't ask you too often.

2. Third-Party Service: Google AdMob

Our app is supported by ads from Google AdMob, which allows us to offer the Service for free.

  • What AdMob Collects: Google may collect data such as your device's Advertising ID, IP address, and other identifiers to serve personalized ads.
  • Google's Policy: We do not control Google's data collection. We recommend you review their policy here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • Opting Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising from Google by visiting your device's "Ads" settings (usually under your device Settings > Google > Ads).

3. Third-Party Service: Google Play In-App Review API

To improve our app, we may use the Google Play In-App Review API to ask for your feedback. This process is managed entirely by Google and is subject to the Google Privacy Policy. We only trigger the request; Google handles the entire review flow, and we do not collect any personal data during this process.

4. Third-Party Service: Google Play In-App Update API

To ensure you have the latest features and security patches, our app uses the Google Play In-App Update API. This service may check with Google Play to see if a new version of our app is available. This process is managed by Google and helps us provide you with a notification about new updates, which you can choose to install.

5. Log Data (For App Stability)

We may collect anonymous information that your device sends ("Log Data") to help us identify and fix crashes. This may include your device's operating system version, app version, and error logs. This data is non-personal and is used only to improve app stability.

Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, although please remember that we do not collect personally identifiable information. For the anonymous Log Data we do collect, we use commercially acceptable means to protect it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Our Service (primarily through ads) may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us. Therefore, we strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of these websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

This Service does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. The content is aimed at Class 10 students, who are in the 13-18 target age group. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information (which should not be possible through the app), please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
